Privacy Policy

Snow Kingdom (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information, especially regarding photos taken for marketing purposes, and outlines your rights as a guest.

Information We Collect
Our website is for informational purposes only. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors online. Visitors cannot submit, upload, comment, or interact with the website in any way. Only website administrators can update site content.
Photography & Media: During your visit to Snow Kingdom, our team may occasionally capture photographs or videos of guests for use in marketing, social media, or promotional materials. We respect your privacy and preferences regarding the use of such media.

Cookies & Tracking
We do not use cookies, analytics, or any other tracking technologies on our website.

Data Sharing
Because we do not collect visitor data through the website, we do not share any personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

❄ Access or view any images in which you appear,
❄ Request removal of your image from our website, social media, or marketing materials (where feasible),
❄ Contact us with any concerns regarding your privacy or the use of your image.
To exercise these rights, please contact us at: contact@snowkingdomphuket.com
